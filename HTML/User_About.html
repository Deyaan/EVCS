Introduction

EV Charging Station App will help the users to see the live Traffic Status.
i.e number of Vehicles waiting to charge their battery in a particular
EV Station.It will help the user to save the time and plan accordingly..

EVCS App is divided into 2 parts:

A]For EV Charging Station Owner
B]For Users

Steps for EV Charging Station which are intrested
in displaying their Traffic Status in my App :

1]Open the EVCS web App in your browser.
2]Click on the EV Station Owner option
3]Do one time Registration
4]Fill the given details accordingly.Two QR Code will be generated for your EV Station.
Check-In QR Code for Entry point and Checkout QR Code for exit point.
5]Save the QR Codes and get a hard copy for the same.
6]Install both the QR Code at the Entry and Exit of your EV Station respectively.
It should be mandatory for each and every Vehicle to scan the QR Code at the time of
Entering the EV Station and at the time of Exiting the EV Station.
It will help to show the accurate Live Traffic Status of your EV Charging Station on the App.

Steps for the Users

1]Open the EVCS web App in your browser.
2]Click on the User option
3]Do one time Registration
4]Go to the Map Page
5]Input your starting point and your Destination.

Results :

All EV Charging Stations coming in the route will be displayed on the screen
with their live Traffic Status
